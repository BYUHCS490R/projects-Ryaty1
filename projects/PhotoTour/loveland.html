Loveland, Colorado

Loveland, Colorado is famous for its sculpture art, especially at the Benson Sculpture Garden, which features nearly 180 bronze sculptures in a beautiful 10-acre park and hosts the annual Sculpture in the Park event. The city has three major foundries that make it a global center for bronze casting art, attracting artists from around the world. Additionally, Loveland has other sculpture parks like Chapungu Sculpture Park with monumental stone sculptures, making it a rich creative hub for outdoor sculpture
