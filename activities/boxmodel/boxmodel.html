Answers the Questions

  1. What is the difference between block tags and inline tags?: Block tags are tags that take up the entire width of the page, while inline tags are tags that take up only the width of the content.
  2. What is the margin, border, and padding?: The margin is the space outside the border, the border is the space between the margin and the padding, and the padding is the space between the border and the content.
  3. What are units of measurement you can use in CSS?: You can use pixels, em, and percent.
  4. When would you use a figure tag?: You would use a figure tag when you want to add a caption to an image.
