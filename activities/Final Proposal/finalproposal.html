Community Nutrition Proposal

1. The title and topic of your site.

The title will be “Community Nutrition”. The topic will address nutrition facts about foods, especially vegetables and fruits that can be found in your community or grown in your backyard.

2. A brief paragraph about why you choose this topic.

Living a healthy life begins with what you eat, and gardening is a passion of mine. This project is an opportunity to gain deeper knowledge about the nutritional qualities of vegetables I often grow at home, and to share this knowledge with others.

3. List out the content you will have on the home page and each of the five pages (one will have a table).

- About Page: explains the site’s purpose, my story, our goals, the value of healthy living and nutrition, and features a table detailing the protein, fat, and calories in different meats.
- Vegetable Pages (4): Each page focuses on a specific vegetable—Beans, Ginger, Garlic, and Roselle Hibiscus—covering nutritional facts, uses, and herbal remedies.
- Sign Up Page: “Support your community.” Visitors can join as members and purchase community-grown vegetables.

4. What fields (or information you will collect) will you put into your form? What data will go on your table?

The sign up form will collect: name, date of birth, phone number, address, email, option to receive updates, agreement checkbox, and sign up button.
The nutritional table will display: meat type, protein (g), fat (g), and calories.

5. How will you collect information/images/content for your site? List at least two of the references you plan on using.

Content will be referenced from reliable sources including:
- USDA
- NutritionFacts.org
Additional academic and government resources will be cited.
