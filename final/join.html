Join the Community Nutrition Members

By joining Community Nutrition, you show support for local gardeners and small growers who share fresh produce and simple nutrition ideas. Members receive occasional updates about seasonal foods, basic nutrition tips, and opportunities to plan regular purchases from nearby growers when available in their area.

This sign-up form helps us understand how to contact you and what type of updates you would like to receive. Your information is used only for this project demonstration and is not stored on any servers.

This community-supported produce program is inspired by examples from local farmers markets and community-supported agriculture groups.
